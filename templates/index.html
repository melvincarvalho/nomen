Nomen

Nomen is an open protocol for global names, like a decentralized DNS, built with Bitcoin and Nostr.

Goals of Nomen:

  • Decentralized: Rules, not rulers!
  • Self-sovereign: You own your identity.
  • Censorship resistant: Anyone can find you.
  • Permissionless: No one can stop you.
